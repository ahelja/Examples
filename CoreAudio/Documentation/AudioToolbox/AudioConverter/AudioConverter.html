AudioConverter.h

Functions for performing audio format conversions.

Includes:

<AvailabilityMacros.h>, <CoreAudio/CoreAudioTypes.h>, <CoreAudioTypes.h>


AudioConverters convert between various linear PCM and compressed audio formats. Supported transformations include:

  • PCM float/integer/bit depth conversions
  • PCM sample rate conversion
  • PCM interleaving and deinterleaving
  • encoding PCM to compressed formats
  • decoding compressed formats to PCM


A single AudioConverter may perform more than one of the above transformations.
